About Me

Hey there! Thanks for visiting my website. I've done a lot in my short 36 years of life, and this is my way of sharing my journey, the things I enjoy, the places I've been, the work I do, and my amazing family I get to continue to do it with.

As an Iowa native, I enjoy the simple things in life, especially the things that help me slow down and smell the flowers. I have a page dedicated to the hobbies I enjoy, so check that out. Perhaps you'll be inspired to take up a new hobby (birding is way more enjoyable than you might think).

If I could travel the world for a living, I am not sure I'd ever get tired of it, but alas, my travel consists of commuting to work. Fortunately, though, I've had the privilege of traveling to amazing places with amazing people, so be sure to check out my travel page.

Since I don't travel for a living, I chose the next best thing: youth director (everyone calls it a youth pastor position except me, so I'll stay on the low road until I am ordained or they change my title). Anyhow, I spend a lot of time teaching middle school and high school students about Jesus and what it looks like to practically follow and trust Him.

The last page of my website is filled with some of my favorite photos from over the years. I have made many memories, and I am forever thankful for the ease of capturing them with a phone camera. There are so many people I've had the privilege of doing life with; I have truly been blessed by their friendship. I hope that sharing the memories that are most important to me will remind you of the people God has placed in your life to create your memories. Thanks for stopping by!
