I tend to stick to what I know, so I am thankful for friends who have invited me along. Check out my photos below of the things I’ve come to enjoy, like hiking, rock climbing, road cycling, disc golf, running, scuba diving, and bird watching!

Birding

My most recent hobby has been bird watching, or ‘birding’ as some like to call it. It’s such a relaxing hobby and has been a great way for me to be outside, enjoy nature, slow down, and step away from screens. Who would’ve thought that listening to bird calls and getting a new ‘lifer’ would be so much fun? I guess that’s why birding has become one of the top hobbies. Bird watching wasn’t enough for me, so I began taking photos, and this is when I really started to enjoy it.
